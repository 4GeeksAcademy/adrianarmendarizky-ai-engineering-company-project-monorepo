From Medellin in 2008 to Brasaland Digital today.

Brasaland started as a family-run grill house and has grown by staying close to guests: quality ingredients, warm service, and meals made with care. Every plate brings a Colombian flair straight from the grill, with bold seasoning, fire-kissed textures, and the familiar comfort of food made to share.

From everyday lunches to weekend gatherings, Brasaland serves delicious grilled food that feels hearty, vibrant, and unmistakably Colombian.
